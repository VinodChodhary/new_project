About Us

KEDIA COMMODITY COMTRADE PVT LTD (KEDIA COMMODITY) is the retail broker that offers all kinds of investment products, such as Equity, Commodity, Currency, Mutual funds and Insurance. Through an excellent online trading platform, Kedia makes it very easy for investors to take control of their investments and diverse their portfolios.

Incorporated in the year 2004, Kedia Commodity Comtrade has been constantly adhering to its principle of safeguarding the interests of investors and business partners. To strengthen our service offering, we established a Research Desk in 2005 that empowers clients with timely information to make the right decisions about Investment.

As a licensed and registered member of various Exchanges such as Multi Commodity Exchange India LTD, National Commodity Derivatives Exchanges Ltd., Kedia is well-aware of the various policies that govern money markets. Small wonder, we serve over 20,000 retail and corporate clients at our 500 franchises.

Vision

To emerge as the one-stop resource and comprehensive solution provider that every investor can contact for all investment needs, support and advice

Mission

To reach out to all those investors (retail and corporate), who wish to maximize their returns and diversify their portfolio in the easiest way To offer research and personalized services that empower investment with timely updates about the investment market, apart from important reports that makes their job a whole lot easier
